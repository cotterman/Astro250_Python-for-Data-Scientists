Description:

Prakash is a 47-year-old married man with three sons from Nepal. After complaining of stomach problems a few months ago, doctors discovered that Prakash had a duodenal perforation, a small hole in his small intestine that could lead to infection, shock, and death without immediate surgery.

Because of the painful tear, Prakash can no longer work. His family now depends on his oldest son’s salary as a school teacher, which barely covers the family’s daily needs. Needless to say, they cannot afford Prakash’s life-saving surgery.

By raising $1,460, we can help Prakash receive surgery to close the duodenal perforation and return to a healthy life with his wife and sons. Let’s make this happen for Prakash!
